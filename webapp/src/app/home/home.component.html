VIRTUAL LABS

Easy and powerful cloud platform



Virtual labs is an interactive platform for managing virtual environments. You can easily get started too by signing up with your institutional email!

You can immediately create a team with the other students in your courses and carry out the projects assigned by the professor with them. All virtual machines are easy to create and manage thanks to the features offered by the platform. Your team's resources are limited, but don't worry! The platform allows you to see how many resources are occupied by your team's virtual machines and how many resources you can still allocate.

Thanks to the integrated section dedicated to assignments, you can easily manage the tasks assigned by the professor, you can also upload your solutions and check your assessments.
